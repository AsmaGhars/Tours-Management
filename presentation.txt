Presentation: 
Our Tour module provides tour operators with a streamlined platform to effortlessly craft, promote, and sell guided tours. 
With a user-friendly "Add Tour" interface, operators can input “Tour” details such as name, destination, duration, and meeting points.
The module's dynamic "New Period" feature facilitates flexible pricing and availability scheduling, while the “Add Image” option enhances visual appeal.
Advanced pricing controls, including child rates and special conditions through the “New Price” and “New Condition” interfaces, cater to diverse market demands.
The “New Location” and ”New Provider”  interfaces facilitate precise geotagging and efficient collaboration.
This module empowers tour operators to showcase their unique offerings and enables travelers to embark on immersive journeys with ease.
